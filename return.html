Return, Policy and Refund

  • 1. Return

    You must have 14 days to return an item from the date you received it. To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. Your item must in the original packing. Your item needs to have the receipt or proof of purchase. In some cases, orders are not eligible for a refund. Before you return an item please contact us.

  • 2. Refund

    Once we receive your item, we will inspect it and notify you that we have received your returned item. we will immediately notify you on the status of your refund after inspecting the item. If your refund is approved, we will initiate a refund to your credit card. You will receive the credit within a certain amount of days, depending on your card issuer's policies. We will make every effort to respond to all refunds as soon as possible. Your patience and understanding are much appreciated. We will contact you if we need any additional information to process your refund. We will guide you through the refund process once the details of your compensation have been confirmed. Your patience is requested while we work topwards resolving the issue for you.
